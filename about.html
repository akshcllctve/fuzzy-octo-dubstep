Lisa Wilson
Founder of The Raw Food Institute


Many of you tell me that you want to eat well, but don't have the support and are confused by different messages. First and foremost, I take a holistic approach to healing and wellness, meaning getting the processed and packaged foods out, and bringing the most nutritiously dense foods in. We will carefully "crowd out" the foods that are making you sick. The good news is that IT WORKS! When people begin to truly nourish their bodies on a cellular level, the RESULTS CAN BE ASTOUNDING. You can literally change your life forever! I believe that everyone deserves to live in a state of blissful health. I am committed to meeting you where you are at, providing you the information, guidance and support that you deserve to bring your body to this natural state of abundant health.

As a national speaker, writer, consultant, business owner, health, nutrition and wellness counselor, fitness trainer, and mom to 3 kids, I love my job because on a daily basis, I guide people to a state of abundant health... with food!

I can help you learn the art and science of living well.
