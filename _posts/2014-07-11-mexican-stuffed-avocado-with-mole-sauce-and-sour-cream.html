recipe by Jillian Love

This mouthwatering south-of-the border entree was a smash hit at the last retreat. Creamy, filling and exquisitely seasoned, this is a great one to try out on your family and friends at the next get-together.

Enjoy!


Stuffed Avocado

1 avocado cut in half for each person
2 cups sunflower seeds (soaked and rinsed)
¼ cup olive oil
1/3 cup red onion finely minced 
1 clove garlic minced 
2 tsp chili powder
2 tsp cumin powder 
½ tsp salt 
1 tbsp white miso paste

Method:
1. pulse everything but the avocado in food processor till it becomes a coarse pate 2. remove pits from avocados and cut in half lengthwise 3. Fill avocado halves with approximately ¼ cup of filling in each 4. Top with Salsa, Mole, Sour Cream, and serve


Sweet Mole Sauce

4 tsp olive oil
3 drops stevia
1 tbsp cashew
2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tbsp coconut water (or water)
¼ cup finely chopped onion
½ cup red pepper
2 tsp oregano
2 tsp ground cumin
2 clove minced garlic
2 tbsp sundried tomato
1 diced hot pepper
½ tsp salt

Method:
1. Blend till smooth 2. Drizzle on top of dish


Sour Cream

1 cup cashews
½ cup young coconut meat
1 tsp raw apple cider vinegar
¼ cup coconut water (or water)
¼ tsp salt
2 tbsp lemon juice

Method:
1. Water as necessary to make a sour cream consistency 2. Blend all ingredients together in a blender until smooth and creamy 3. Drizzle on top of dish


Chipotle Cheddar Crumble

1 cup cashews (soaked one hour and rinsed)
1 orange bell pepper
1/4 cups nutritional yeast
juice of 2 lemons
½ tsp chipotle powder

Method
1. Blend all ingredients till smooth 2. Spread on Teflex sheet ¼ inch thick 3. Dehydrate 2 hours at 105 then peel off sheet and continue drying on teflex tray with screen 4. Dehydrate another 6-8 hours or till dry and crumbly 5. Sprinkle on top of dish
