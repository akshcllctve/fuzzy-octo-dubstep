Ingredients:

2 ½ c. raw almond butter
1 ½ c. raw honey or coconut nectar
2 c. raw cacao powder
3 tb. raw maca powder
pinch sea salt or Himalayan salt
1-2 c. raw cacao nibs, shredded coconut, chopped almonds, or whatever you would like to roll them in...

Combine the first 5 ingredients in a bowl. Mix thoroughly, so that the mixture becomes a smooth, fairly firm dough. Scoop out little balls, roll them between your hands so they are nice and round, and roll in your topping of choice... Makes 35-40 truffles.
