SMART, make ahead salad dressings, for the week! DONE! A salad a day keeps the doctor away! We eat salads here for lunch daily. I used to make dressings fresh daily. Now, to save time I make them up and eat for 7-10 days. With at least one salad a day, 5 family members and sometimes guests, we are pushing out a minimum of 35 salad per week, at our house!

1.) Wilson's Mock Caesar Dressing


Mock Caesar Dressing

2 cups olive oil
1 1/4 cup lime juice
1/4 cup water
3 Tbs dijon mustard
1 Tbs worcestershire
3 Tbs ground flax
1/2 cup pine nuts
1/2 cup walnuts
5 cloves garlic

Method: Blend in vitamix on high. Massage into your kale and enjoy!

2.) Wilson's house Vinaigrette

House Vinaigrette

2 cups olive oil
1 1/2 cups, freshly squeezed lemon
3 inches of ginger root, peeled and chopped before blending (less if you don't like spicy)
4 cloves freshly peeled garlic

Method: Blend on high. Toss over any salad.

Ideas to switch this up:
1. Add fresh herbs (we love cilantro and parsley)
2. Change the citrus base from lemon to lime, orange, grapefruit or pomegranate juice
3. Add nuts like walnuts or brazil nuts to give this a creamy taste
4. Add nutritional yeast for a cheesy mouth taste
Both of these dressings will keep 1-2 weeks in the refrigerator.
And that is your time saving tip!
Enjoy!
