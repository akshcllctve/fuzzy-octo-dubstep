Morning smoothies

Here is how I like to start the morning sometimes, it takes  2 oranges, peeled, almond mylk (see my note below), cinnamon, maca (Maca has long been used to increase stamina and combat fatigue, from the Andes ), vanilla, chaga (a mushroom with medicinal properties) , 1/3 frozen banana & some ice. Blend, pour into a glass  & enjoy! - Lisa

Making almond mylk is easy!

  • You need a blender, some straining tools (cheese cloth), a second finer mesh strainer (fine muslin bag) for your second pass and a bowl.
  •   a cup of almond nuts to 3 cups of water.  A pound of nuts makes about a half gallon of almond mylk
  • Soak over night and blend a cup at a time or so.
