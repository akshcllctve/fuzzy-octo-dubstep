Our am RISE & SHINE ROUTINE:

  • 1.) Get up, prepare a green breakfast, with some kind of healthy fat.
  • 2.) Pack power lunch: Always a salad, of some variety to put in their lunch boxes. Add snacks (pieces of fruit) & spring water. Salad usually has: sprouts, leaves, sometimes a fruit, goji berries, & seeds or nuts such as hemp, sesame, sunflower, pumpkin, brazil, walnut, almond, jungle peanut etc. This is usually topped with a home made vinaigrette, such as fresh garlic, ginger, EVOO, ACV or lemon juice.
  • 3.) Off they go to catch the bus! (((BTW: Never sick & not a cavity among the 3--& never, ever had fluoride in water, paste or gel.)))
