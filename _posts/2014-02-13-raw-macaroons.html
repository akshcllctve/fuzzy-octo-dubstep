These decadent raw macaroons are the perfect way to treat yourself and your loved ones. Healthy, delicious and RAW!

Raw Macaroons

3 cups unsweetened raw coconut flakes
1.5 cups raw cacao powder
1 cup maple syrup
1/3 cup coconut butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp sea salt

Method:

Mix in a large mixing bowl. Place in small cookie type shapes and dehydrate at 105 degrees for 12-24 hours. When it is slightly crisp on the outside & chewy in the middle it is done.
