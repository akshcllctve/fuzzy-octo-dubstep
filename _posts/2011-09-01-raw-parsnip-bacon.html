Raw Parsnip Bacon

Recipe Type: Breakfast
Author: Kenzie Harrick
Prep time: 10 mins
Cook time: 12 hours
Total time: 12 hours 10 mins
Serves: 4
A vegetable bacon? Not quite the traditional bacon (good thing!) but has a great smokey and crisp flavor!
Ingredients
  • 8 Parsnips
  • 3 Tbs olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Dehydrator
Instructions
  1. Shred parsnips with vegetable peeler into long strips.
  2. Coat with olive oil and salt.
  3. Dehydrate on 105 degrees for 12 hours.
