Over the past three years, here at The Raw Food Institute, we are honored to have touched thousands of lives.

Hundreds of people come to our speaking events and retreats every quarter, and are achieving healthier, happier living through a plant based, raw food diet. This year our goal is to raise awareness even further, into the mainstream.

The message is getting out, and people are starting to know somebody, or know somebody who knows somebody who healed their body, or just felt completely different in a profound way by incorporating green juice and raw food into their life.

I mean really, thats what its all about. We want everyone to feel this good inside and step into their best life.
