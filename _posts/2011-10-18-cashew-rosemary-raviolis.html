Cashew Rosemary Raviolis
#ratingval# from #reviews# reviews
Print
Recipe Type: Main
Author: Raw Chef Chelly
Prep time: 25 mins
Total time: 25 mins
Serves: 4
A delicious raw ravioli that can be used as a base for any season or flavor!
Ingredients
  • 1 daikon radish
  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 Tbs fresh rosemary
Instructions
  1. Slice daikon on a Mandolin into circles.
  2. Blend filling ingredients until smooth.
  3. Scoop 1 tsp of cheeze onto ravioli, top with a second slice of daikon.
