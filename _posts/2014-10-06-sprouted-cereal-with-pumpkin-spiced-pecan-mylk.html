During the month of October I will be focusing on the theme "Fall Food For You!" I love this time of year and it is a great excuse for me to get in the kitchen and create seasonal recipes.

This week I have decided to share my go to cereal for busy mornings. I like to make ahead and have for the entire week. To spice it up I have created a Pumpkin Spice Pecan Mylk that is so delicious! Pumpkins have over the top health benefits! They are loaded with vitamins, carotenes and anti-oxidants and the list goes on. Enjoy this recipe as the weather changes!

Lisa's Sprouted Cereal

1 cup sprouted buckwheat
½ cup sprouted pumpkin seeds
1 cup sprouted sunflower seeds
¾ cup sesame seeds
1 cup sprouted chopped walnuts
½ cup goji berries or dried fruit of choice
½ tsp salt
Sprinkle with Cinnamon

Method:
Combine all ingredients and mix together. It is that easy!!

Pumpkin Spiced Pecan Mylk

1 Cups Soaked Pecans
4 Cups Water
½ Cup Sugar Pumpkin Pumpkin peeled, seeded and cubed
1/2 tsp Cinnamon
1/4 tsp Ground Ginger
1/8 tsp Ground Cloves

Method:
1. Combine all ingredients in vita-mix and blend on high.
2. Strain with a nutmylk bag squeezing to get all of the liquid out.
3. Refrigerate.

Enjoy!
