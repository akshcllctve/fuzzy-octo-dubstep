Raw Nori Wraps

several pieces of raw nori paper

cauliflower rice: 1/2 cup cauliflower, 3 Tbs pine nuts: blended on low

slice avocado

shredded cucumbers

shredded carrots

sprouts

3 Tbs raw and fermented sauerkraut

1 Tbs water for nori adhesive

Place vegetables on nori wrap, closest to you. Roll the Nori paper away from your body, hugging the vegetables snuggly. Wet your finger and run along the edge of the nori paper to seal the roll. Enjoy!
