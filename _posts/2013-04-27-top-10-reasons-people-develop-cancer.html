Top 10 reasons people develop cancer

Are you at risk? Top reason's people develop cancer:

  • 1. Smoking
  • 2. Immediate family member with cancer
  • 3. EMF exposure
  • 4. Consumption of red meat
  • 5. Low consumption of Fruit & vegetables
  • 6. Obesity
  • 7. Above average sugar consumption
  • 8. Depression
  • 9. Diesel emissions
  • 10. Dairy (> 1 serving per day)
  • 11. Refined Flour intake
  • 12. Using Omega 6 polyunsaturate oils-corn, safflower, sunflower & soy oil
  • 13. Alcohol consumption

This from: Dr. Michael Murray, Dr. Tim Birdsall, Dr. Joseph Pizzorno & Dr. Paul Reilly, authors of:: "How to Prevent & Treat Cancer with Natural Medicine."
