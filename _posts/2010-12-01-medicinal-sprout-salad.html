Lisa's Favorite Dressing

1/8 cup lemon juice

3 Tbs olive oil

1 Tbs Nama Shoyu

1 tsp kep flakes

2 in square ginger

1 clove garlic

1 tsp turmeric

black pepper to taste

Blend and pour over salad.

Sprout Salad:

1 cup mixed sprouts

1 cups shredded cabbage

1/2 cup chopped cilantro

1/2 cup chopped walnuts

1 chopped cucumber

sesame seeds

sliced avocado

Combine all chopped ingredients. Serve with dressing.
