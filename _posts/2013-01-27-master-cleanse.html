The Master Cleanse is a modified fast that permits the use of lemon, maple syrup and cayenne pepper.

Lisa's modified version:

  • 7am 1tbsp epsom salt in warm water
  • 8am get clean tea through day, blended in VM, lemon with seeds, pith & 1/4 of the skin still on, 1 tsp maple syrup, pinch cayenne pepper, water...
  • 9pm calming bed time tea, I will also add some green juices.

I'll do this until my body says stop. Last time: 5 days of feeling fantastic! p.s.: This is not for everyone. Consult your health professional before doing this, or any cleanse...
