Simple Raw Miso Soup
Recipe Type: Soup
Author: Kenzie Harrick
Prep time: 10 mins
Total time: 10 mins
Serves: 2
A simple recipe that's almost identical to the Traditional Japanese Soup
Ingredients
  • broth:
  • 2 Tbs Miso
  • 3 cups warm, filterd water
  • garnish:
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup jicama chopped in small cubes
  • 2 Tbs Dulse, chopped small
  • 1 cup marinated mushrooms, chopped thin (optional)
Instructions
  1. Blend miso and warm water together.
  2. Top with garnish ingredients
  3. Serve warm
