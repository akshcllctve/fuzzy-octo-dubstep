I sometimes indulge in this raw breakfast of sprouted buckwheat, sprouted pepitos, chia seeds, help seeds, walnuts, goji berries and some fresh fruit--here green apple, but berries are fantastic. Delicious start to the day!

Always soak all seeds , especially the chia seeds to avoid constipation.
