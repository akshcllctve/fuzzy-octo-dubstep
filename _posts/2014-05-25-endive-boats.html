This recipe is a no brainer for summer gatherings. It's simple, healthy, filling, delicious and so colorful that if you are entertaining this impressive recipe is sure to wow your guests. The best part is every single ingredient is a living food!

I am always looking for ways excuses to use different lettuce options in the store. I saw some beautiful endive boats in the market and my creative juices started spinning.

In order to really take this recipe to the next level I decided to include our sprouted hummus recipe.


1. In vita-mix combine the following ingredients and blend on high for 1 minute

Hummus

2 Cups Sprouted Chickpeas
2 Tablespoons Blonde Sesame Seeds
1 Teaspoon Sea Salt
1/2 Cup Cold Pressed Olive Oil
Juice of 2 Lemons
4 Garlic Cloves
Pinch of Paprika to Garnish

Blend all ingredients oh high in Vita-mix for 1 minute *Keep water close in case it is too thick - adjust as necessary



2. Peel apart the each endive individually (they should look like little boats) and wash them


3. Fill each endive boat with a generous amount of sprouted hummus


4. Slice the following vegetables in matchstick slices

Cucumber
Carrot
Beet
Broccoli (stem only)
Daikon Radish
Red Pepper


5. Layer the matchstick slices in the endive boats alternating each vegetable for maximum color variation
