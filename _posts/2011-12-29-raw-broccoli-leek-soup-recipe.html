Recipe Type: Soup
Author: Lisa Wilson
Prep time: 10 mins
Total time: 10 mins
Serves: 4
A great treat in the cooler times of the year
Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup pine-nuts
  • 1-2 medium leeks
  • 1 small head of broccoli
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup of cashews
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 cups spring water
  • 1 Tsp sea salt
  • 1 Tbs nutritional yeast
Instructions
  1. Use a large blender to mix all ingredients except salt
  2. Start low, blend 1-2 minutes, just warm to the touch. Not above 105F !
  3. Serve with a sprinkle of sea salt
  4. Enjoy!
