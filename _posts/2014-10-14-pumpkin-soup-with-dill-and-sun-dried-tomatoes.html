This week I bring you another pumpkin recipe. This soup is slightly warmed in the vita-mix and and makes for a perfect fall comfort food. Enjoy this soup on a chilly fall day!

Pumpkin Soup with Dill and Grated Sun-dried Tomatoes

2 Cups Sugar Pumpkin, cubed and peeled
3 Cups Coconut Water
1 Cup Pecans
2 Stalks Celery
¼ Sweet Onion
¼ Lemon with Skin if Organic
1 Clove Garlic
1 Cube Ginger Peeled
3 Sun-dried Tomatoes
1 tsp Apple Cider Vinegar
¼ tsp Sea Salt
2 tsp Cinnamon
Dash Curry Powder and Turmeric
Grated Sun-dried Tomatoes for Garnish
Chopped Fresh Dill for Garnish
Drizzle Olive Oil for Garnish

Method:
1. Peel pumpkin and cube
2. Combine pumpkin, coconut water, pecans, celery, onion, lemon, garlic, ginger, sun-dried tomatoes, apple cider vinegar, sea salt, cinnamon, curry powder and turmeric in the vita-mix and blend on high for 2 minutes.
3. Garnish with grated sun-dried tomatoes, dill and olive oil

Enjoy!
