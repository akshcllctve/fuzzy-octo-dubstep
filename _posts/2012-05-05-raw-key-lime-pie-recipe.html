Here is a wonderful recipe from Margaret Rizzuto for a raw key lime pie

Raw Key Lime Pie Recipe

Recipe Type: Desert
Author: Margaret Rizzuto
Prep time: 3 hours
Total time: 3 hours
Serves: 6
This tasty desert has a raw macadamia and cashew nut crust with a wonderful raw key lime filling
Ingredients
  • Macadamia / Cashew Nut Crust:
  • 1 1/4 c Macadamia Nuts
  • 1/4 c Cashews
  • 2 T Coconut Oil, melted (put coconut oil in a glass then submerge in hot water to melt)
  • 2 T Agave Nectar
  • Pinch of Celtic Sea Salt
  • Filling
  • 4 Avocado cut into quarters
  • 1/2 c Lime juice
  • 1/2 c Agave Nectar
  • 1/2 c Coconut Oil, melted
  • 1 T Lime zest – can add a little more if you like a bit more zing.
Instructions
  1. For crust, process the nuts in the food processor until they form a slightly crunchy meal. Add the coconut oil and salt and continue to process until blended but still a bit chopped. Place into a pie pan with removeable bottom and firmly press crust into the pan. Place in the freezer for about an hour.
  2. For the filling: Add all the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth and easy to pour. Be sure not to over blend. Pour filling into the crust, then freeze approx. 1-2 hours or overnight to set. To serve top with a little extra lime zest, or shredded coconut.
