My comfort food since I was a child was warm milk with walnuts in it. Someone once told me this was an eastern European tradition. I don't drink dairy milk anymore because it creates mucous in my ears, but I still crave warm drinks on a wintry day, so I came up with this velvety, milky drink alternative. Warming, comforting, delicious, and takes me back to my childhood memories every time :)

Enjoy!

Lisa Wilson

Winter Comfort Drink

1/3 cup coconut mylk
2/3 cup almond mylk
1/8 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp freshly ground nutmeg
1/8 tsp fresh vanilla bean, or vanilla extract
Method:

Blend on high in Vitamix, until warm or slightly warm on stove. Shave some fresh nutmeg on top for a pretty touch. Delight & Enjoy!~
