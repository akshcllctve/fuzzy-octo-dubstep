I've been making this with all kinds of fruits. Blueberries, peaches, mangoes etc etc etc Love this one!

Mangoes with raw vanilla pudding:

  • 2 pears, peeled
  • 1 tbsp vanilla bean
  • 1 cup cashews, or macadamia nuts
  • blend in Vitamix, on high until smooth
  • 4 tbsp of coconut oil

top with raw cereal:

  • sprouted & dehydrated buckwheat
  • chopped brazil nuts
  • black sesame seeds
  • goji berries
  • cinnamon

ENJOY!!!
