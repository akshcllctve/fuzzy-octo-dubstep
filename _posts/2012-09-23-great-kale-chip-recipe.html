Me too! I can honestly say that a great kale chip can satisfy that desire. Great Kale chip recipe: Blend in Vitamix:
  • 1 bag bag cashews (as an indulgence!)
  • 1 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1 red bell pepper-seeds removed
  • 1 whole lemon peeled
  • juice of one lemon
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast (optional)
  • Blend and rub onto your kale
  • Dehydrate & enjoy!
btw, this is great on an onion too for crispy onion chips! Once you get this down, you can do spicy chili, or cilantro lime..... Have some fun with this! YUM!
