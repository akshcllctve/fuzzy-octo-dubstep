Fennel and Pomegranate Salad
Recipe Type: Salad
Author: Lisa Wilson
Prep time: 10 mins
Total time: 10 mins
A quick and easy raw salad featuring pomegranates and fennel
Ingredients
  • 4-5 fennel
  • 3 red onion
  • 5-6 oranges
  • 3 pomegranates
  • olive oil
  • sea salt
  • black pepper
Instructions
  1. chop the pomegranates, onions, fennel, oranges
  2. lightly toss in:
  3. olive oil
  4. sea salt
  5. black pepper
  6. Serve
