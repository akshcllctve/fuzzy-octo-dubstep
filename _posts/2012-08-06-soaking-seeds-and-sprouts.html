Soaking seeds and sprouts.  I get asked a lot on  how much room sprouting takes up and how to sprout beans and legumes.

Soak your sprouts overnight, Put in about one inch of dried beans or legumes and leave on counter until morning.

Rinse your sprouts in the morning and drain, rinse every few hours and drain again. Continue for 48 hours until your sprout tails are twice the size of the body.  After the sprouts have drained for a few hours, change to a proper lid, without holes.   Will last about two weeks in the refrigerator.
