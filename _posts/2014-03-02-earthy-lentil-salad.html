So simple you won't believe how great this one tastes. Filling, crunchy sprouted lentils, fresh tomatos, creamy avocados; this salad has it all. One of our all-time favorite salad recipes - seriously, this is our go to salad, we eat it at least once a week.

Enjoy!

Lisa Wilson

Earthy Lentil Salad

4 cups sprouted lentils
2 tomatos chopped in big chunks
1/2 purple onion chopped
drizzle of olive oil (about 1/4 cup)
2 avocados cut in half
Sea Salt to taste
Black Pepper to taste
Method:

Prepare all the ingredients and mix. Serve over the avocado halves. That's it! Enjoy!~
