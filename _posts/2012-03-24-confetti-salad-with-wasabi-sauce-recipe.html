Confetti Salad with Wasabi Sauce

Recipe Type: Entree
Author: Margaret Rizzuto
Prep time: 10 mins
Total time: 10 mins
Serves: 2
A colorful blend of nutrient rich vegetables that will capture the attention of almost anyone. The nice 'kick' that comes from the wasabi contains a rich source of chemicals known as isothiocyanates. These are the same anti-cancer chemicals found in broccoli and cabbage. Wasabi is also know for its anti-inflammatory properties.
Ingredients
  • 1 jicama
  • 1-2 zucchini
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 large beet
  • 1/4 head of purple cabbage
  • Dressing
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 T apple cider vinegar
  • 3 T nama shoyu
  • 2 T honey
  • 1/2 t wasabi powder
Instructions
  1. Run all vegetables through a mandoline set for thin julienne (alternatively, veggies could be julienned with a knife). Toss to mix colors, then add dressing and garnish with sesame seeds.
  2. Blend ingredients well, taste and add a bit more wasabi for more 'kick'.
  3. Garnish
  4. Black & white sesame seeds
