Cinnamon Apple Crisps:

5 Green apples

1 Tbs Cinnamon

-cut apples into paper thin round chips.

-sprinkle with cinnamon and line on dehydrator trays

-dehydrate at 105 degrees for 12 hours

-Enjoy!
