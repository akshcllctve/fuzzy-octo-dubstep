Lisa Wilson of The Raw Food Institute prepares a red pepper spaghetti sauce with vegetable noodles using a spirooli slicer, all live all raw!

Spaghetti at The Raw Food Institute
Author: Lisa Wilson The Raw Food Institute
Recipe type: Entree
Prep time:  10 mins
Total time:  10 mins
Serves: 4
 
A great tasty substitute for spaghetti when your craving the real thing.
Ingredients
  • 1½ Cups raw red pepper
  • 2 Tbs Chopped Onion
  • ½ Cup lemon juice
  • 2 Tbs Olive oil (cold pressed, organic)
  • 2 Tbs. Nama shoyu soy sauce
  • 1 Clove garlic
  • 2 Zucchini or squash for noodles
Instructions
  1. Set aside the Zucchini or squash for noodles
  2. Blend all the other ingredients in a high speed blender until warm, but not above 105 F
  3. Peel zucchini or squash for the noodles and slice using a spirooli slicer
