Thought I'd share: 2 fresh oranges peeled 4 cups ice 1/4 to 1/2 cup fresh almond milk 1/2 tsp vanilla bean, or vanilla extract raw honey, or maple syrup to taste  

Blend in Vitamix on high, pushing sides down with tamper until completely blended.  This will likely only take 30 seconds or so to blend!  Then simply enjoy!
