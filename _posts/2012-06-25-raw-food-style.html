Too much to do for that, so today I'll go with the lite bites of nutrients! Perfect to keep me going all afternoon!  Raw Food Style. These are endive boats with seed "meat" and veggies. Super yum! Fill me up, without weighing me down!  I enjoy that with my green juice. In Vitamix, combine sunflower seeds, cashews, red pepper, juice of one or 2 lemons.  If you like a cheesy taste, you can also add nutritional yeast.  You will need a total of about 3 cups of raw cashews & sunflower seeds.  Refrigerate the left overs & enjoy for one week. Green juice I'm enjoying today:  celery, cucumber, pea shoots, lime, collard greens.  Juice & enjoy!
