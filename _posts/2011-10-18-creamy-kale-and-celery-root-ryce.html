Creamy Kale and Celery Root Ryce
#ratingval# from #reviews# reviews
Print
Recipe Type: Side Dish
Author: Raw Chef Chelly
Prep time: 15 mins
Total time: 15 mins
Serves: 6
A delicious autumn meal with bursting flavor!
Ingredients
  • 2 cups celery root, pureed in a food processor
  • 1 bunch Kale, washed and torn or julienned
  • 1/4 cup hemp seeds, garnish
  • Cream Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Tahini
  • 1/4 olive oil
  • 1/2 bunch fresh dill
  • 1/4-1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 lemon, juiced
Instructions
  1. Puree celery root in food processor until you make a rice texture. Mix celery root ryce and kale with cream sauce. Garnish with hemp seeds.
