Italian wraps, for dinner

Using your mandolin, cut match stick slices of yellow squash. Mix these with kelp noodles. With spine removed, roll sauce & 'noodles' into a collard wrap.

Italian red sauce recipe:

  • 1 package sun dried tomatoes
  • 2 fresh tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp raw sweet onion
  • 3 cloves raw garlic
  • 2 tbsp nama shoyu
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
 

  • Blend on high in Vitamix, until thoroughly mixed.
  • Serve with your favorite side salad. Enjoy!
