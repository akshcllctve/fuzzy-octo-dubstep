Raw Banana Pancake Recipe
#ratingval# from #reviews# reviews
Print
Recipe Type: Breakfast
Author: Kenzie Harrick
Prep time: 5 mins
Cook time: 12 hours
Total time: 12 hours 5 mins
Serves: 2-3
When you're craving brunch, these raw banana pancakes hit the spot!
Ingredients
  • 5 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup shredded dried coconut
  • 1/2 cup ground flax seeds
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • pinch salt
  • 1 Tbs cacoa powder or cinnamon (optional)
  • fresh or frozen fruit and nuts (optional)
  • Dehydrator
Instructions
  1. Mash or puree bananas with flax and coconut.
  2. If using cocoa or cinnamon, add those also.
  3. Stir in any fruit or nuts.
  4. Dehydrate at 110 degrees overnight, 12-16 hours.
