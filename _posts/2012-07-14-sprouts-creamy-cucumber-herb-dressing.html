Sprouts & Creamy Cucumber Salad Recipe

A great raw food recipe for the summer time with a light course of sprouts & creamy cucumber.Â  The 'cream' comes from the almond mylk. Blend about 2 inches of cucumber with some fresh almond mylk. Stir in chopped cucumber & dried thyme. This one has no oil, but adding some hemp oil, or oo is optional! Serve on top of freshly clipped buckwheat & sunflower sprouts! Enjoy!
