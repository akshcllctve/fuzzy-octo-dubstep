Carob Hazelnut Truffles
Recipe Type: Dessert
Author: Margaret Rizzuto
Prep time: 20 mins
Total time: 20 mins
Serves: 6-8
Carob helps to regulate the digestion process, while serving as a natural anti-allergic, antiseptic, and anti-bacterial agent. It contains tannins that are rich in gallic acid, which give it its beneficial effects. Additionally, carob provides powerful anti-viral benefits that supply the body with essential antioxidants.
Ingredients
  • 1 cup of hazelnuts
  • 4 T Carob
  • 2 T coconut oil
  • 1/8 t sea salt
Instructions
  1. Process the nuts in the food processor until completely chopped.
  2. Add the dates, sea salt, coconut oil and carob powder. Process again until everything is well mixed.
  3. Taste your mixture and add a little more Carob if needed.
  4. Roll the mixture in your hand to form balls. Don’t be concerned if it seems a little soft. Once refrigerted it will firm up beautfully.
  5. If you’d like to can roll the balls in shredded coconut, carob powder, or finely diced goji berries – have fun & be creative.
  6. Refrigerate a few hours so truffles can set before serving – enjoy!
  7. Makes 6-8 Truffles depending on size
