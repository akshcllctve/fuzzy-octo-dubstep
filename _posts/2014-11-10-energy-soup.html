You can eat raw foods all winter long with warm raw soups! Use the Vitamix to slowly warm your soups while blending. Try this healing Energy Soup!

Energy Soup

Yield: 8-9 cups

2 cups green juice
2 cups filtered water
1 cup spinach
½ cucumber
½ red bell pepper
1 stalk celery
1 ½ cups sunflower sprouts
½ avocado
1 Tbs dulse flakes
1 clove garlic
1 tsp fresh ginger

Method: In a blender, combine all ingredients. Blend well and season to taste.

Enjoy!
