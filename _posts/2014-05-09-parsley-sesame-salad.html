Let the flavor of the parsley shine through with this simple, fresh and healthy recipe. Sesame seeds are a very good source of manganese and copper as well as calcium, magnesium, iron, phosphorus, vitamin B1, zinc and dietary fiber.




Salad

1 Bunch Parsley
1 Bunch Spinach
1 Bunch Kale
5 Scallions
1 ½ Cups White Sesame Seeds

Chop the greens and mix everything together



Dressing

¾ Cup Olive Oil
3T Nama Shoyu
Lemon Juice
2 Cloves Garlic

Peel the garlic, combine and blend on high in vita-mix for 30 seconds

Enjoy!
