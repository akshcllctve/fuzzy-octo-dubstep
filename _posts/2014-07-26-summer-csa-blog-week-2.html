Romaine Antipasto Boats

½ Head Romaine
1 Red Pepper
1 Yellow Pepper
Chopped fresh chives

Method:

1. Rip off the entire leaves of romaine leaving the whole leaf intact
2. Wash the romaine
3. Chop the peppers and chives
4. Lay out romaine leaf open face
5. Fill romaine with Lemon Sunflower Pate
5. Top with peppers and chives
6. Drizzle carrot ginger cream dressing over

Lemon Sunflower Pate

2 cups raw sunflower seeds (soaked for 2 hours and rinsed)
1/4 cupfreshly squeezed lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/2 to 1 teaspoon sea salt

Method:

Place all pate ingredients into a food processor fitted with the "s" blade and pulse until desired consistency

Carrot Ginger Cream Dressing

2C fresh carrots
½ C chopped cashews
¼ Clemon juice
½ C olive oil
¼ Cwater
5-10 drops stevia
1T fresh ginger juice
¼ t turmeric juice or 1 tsp turmeric powder
¼ t cayenne
1 clove garlic
1 t salt
1 t black pepper

Method:

1. Blend together till blended but still slightly coarse

‘Caesar’ Salad

Romaine chopped

Method:

1. Wash the romaine
2. Chop the romaine
3. Toss with Caesar Dressing
4. Add Almond Parmesan
5. Add Caesar Dressing and Almond Parmesan

Caesar Dressing

½ cup raw sesame tahini
¼ cup hijiki, arame or wakame soaked to hydrate
1 clove garlic
2 tb. dulse flakes
½ tsp. mustard powder
⅔ cup water
2 tb. extra virgin olive oil
1 tb. fresh lemon juice
1 tb. coconut aminos

Method:

1. In a blender, combine all ingredients. Blend well and season to taste.

Almond Parmesan

Yield: 3 cups
3 cups almond meal
2 clove garlic
1 T. dried oregano or pizza seasoning
¼ cup extra virgin olive oil

Method:

1. In a food processor, using the S-blade, combine the almond meal, garlic, and oregano. Process to a fine powder.
2. Add the olive oil and Braggs. The mixture should stick together slightly when pressed.
3. Season to taste and serve.

NOTE: Almond meal is the pulp leftover from the making of almond milk. Take what you have strained out from making the milk and dehydrate it. It can be used in pate, dessert, and to make such goodies as this parmesan!

Kale and Golden Beet Salad with Red Peppers with Herbed Dressing

1 Bunch Kale
1 Golden Beet
1 Bunch Scallions cut ½ way
1 Red Pepper
½ Cup Pea Shoots
⅓ Cup Hemp Seeds

Method:

1. Thinly slice cabbage, radish, turnip, carrot, and spring onion. You can do this with a knife or cuisinart
2. Toss with Herbed dressing
3. Top with dressing
4. Garnish with sesame seeds and cilantro

Herbed Dressing

½ Cup Olive Oil
¼ Cup Apple Cider Vinegar
2T Tamari Soy Sauce
3T Sesame Seeds
2 Cloves Garlic
1 Sprig Fresh Oregano
8-10 Leaves Fresh Parsley

Asian Inspired ‘Slaw’

1 head green cabbage
1 bunch red radish
1 bunch turnips
3-5 carrots
1 bunch green tops of spring onion
2T white sesame seeds
Cilantro to garnish

Ginger Dressing

2 T apple cider vinegar
2 ½ T sesame oil (I used a chile one to add some spice!)
2 ½ T olive oil
¼ cup lime juice
2T nama shoyu
1T raw honey
1 inch piece of ginger
1 garlic cloves
Dash of miso

Vinegar and Sea Salt Raw Potato Chips

3-4 regular potatoes
Raw apple cider vinegar, e.g. Bragg
Himalayan crystal salt

Method

1. Place potatoes (peeled + sliced) in a large bowl with enough apple cider vinegar to cover them.
2. soak for 10-15 minutes.
3. Drain chips and lay flat on mesh screen
4. Sprinkle with salt.
5. Dehydrate at 105°F for 5-10 hours or until crispy.
