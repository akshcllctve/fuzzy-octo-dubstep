Lisa Wilson's Holiday Salad

1 or 2 fennel bulbs: Slice the white fennel bulb into long, thin slices. Cut the thin green tops that look like dill off and set aside for garnish.
2 purple onion: Slice into long, thin slices.
5 oranges: Cut into long thin slices, or pinwheels and them quarter your pinwheels to make bite size pieces.
4 pomegranate: Peel and seed to toss into this salad.
Dressing: Organic, cold pressed olive oil.

Adjust the amounts above to suit your palate and preferences. Crack some mineral salt, such as Celtic or Himalayan and pepper over this salad to enhance the flavor. Serve and watch your guests go crazy for this dish!
