Raw Food Educator Program


Become a Certified Raw Food Educator (RFE) and learn the tools you need to successfully and confidently coach your clients in raw food nutrition, detox, and healing.


As a Certified Raw Food Educator (RFE) you will be able to:

  • Counsel individuals on adopting a healing raw food diet
  • Help people on their personal healing journey with raw foods
  • Write articles, books, magazine articles and other literature on raw food nutrition
  • Teach raw foods “un-cooking” classes
  • Hold group classes on the powers of raw foods
  • Go into people’s homes for one-to-one raw food coaching

Lecture Topics Include:

  • Working With Clients Meeting the client where they're at, walking the walk, how to deal with illness and loving what you do.
  • Finding Your Niche Where can raw foods take you? How to be an educator and working with healing groups.
  • Organizing Your Raw Food Business Do you need to incorporate? How to handle finances, organizing your office and client folders, staying motivated, setting goals and making your vision a reality.
  • Developing a Client's Raw Food Program How to group the Raw Food Institute protocol into a well organized learning structure for your clients, group classes, or those you will be working with.
  • Marketing, Gathering Clients, How to Close and What to Charge Developing the right language around healing and sales, good marketing tools and strategies to make sure your investment pays off.
